Privacy policy v0.1

Data collection

We may collect some of your publicly listed information from your Discord profile to improve your experience. We do not collect any of your private information or information that is supposed to be accessible to certain individuals (like messages).

  • Data we collect about YOU:
    1. Your user ID (used for database identification).
    2. Time of the first interaction with the bot.
    3. Time of your last interaction with the bot (used for detecting inactive users).
  • Data we collect about YOUR SERVER:
    1. Server ID (used for database identification).
  • Data we LOG (for debugging):
    1. All of your command/ui interactions.
Data usage

Your data is not being used outside our application. Everything stored in the database is only accessible to the current stable version of the application. Testing is being done on a separate database.

Your private data is not accessible by other users. Your data can be completely erased by your request.

  • Data we consider PRIVATE:
    1. Your settings & configurations.
    2. Time of your first & last interaction with the bot.
  • Data we consider PUBLIC:
    1. Your Discord ID & username.
    2. Your activity & status.
    3. Discord account creation time.

Some of your public data is not being collected, however it still may be used by the application when needed.
